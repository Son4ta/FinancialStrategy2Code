Strategy Name: Simple Moving Average (SMA) Golden Cross

This strategy is a classic trend-following strategy designed to capture long-term upward trends.

- Asset: Apple Inc. (AAPL)
- Data Frequency: Daily OHLCV (Open, High, Low, Close, Volume) data.
- Indicators:
  1. 50-day Simple Moving Average (short-term SMA).
  2. 200-day Simple Moving Average (long-term SMA).
- Position Sizing: Use 95% of the portfolio's cash for each trade.
- Entry Rule (Long):
  - A "Golden Cross" occurs when the 50-day SMA crosses ABOVE the 200-day SMA.
  - When this signal happens, buy the asset.
- Exit Rule (Long):
  - A "Death Cross" occurs when the 50-day SMA crosses BELOW the 200-day SMA.
  - When this signal happens, sell all positions to go flat (back to cash).
- Other rules:
  - The strategy only goes long. No short selling.
  - Only one position is held at a time.
